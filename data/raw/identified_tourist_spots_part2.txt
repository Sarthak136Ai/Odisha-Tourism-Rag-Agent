# Additional Identified Tourist Centres in Odisha

This document lists the additional officially identified tourist centers in Odisha organized by district, place name, and its significance or importance.

## Malkangiri District Tourist Centres
* **Balimela** — Scenic Spot.
* **Chitrakonda** — Scenic Spot.
* **Malkangiri** — District Headquarters.
* **Motu** — Scenic Spot.
* **Raja-Rani Bandha** — Scenic Spot.
* **Satiguda** — Scenic Spot.

## Mayurbhanj District Tourist Centres
* **Bangiriposi** — Strategic Point.
* **Baripada** — District Headquarters, famous for Chhau dance.
* **Bhimakunda** — Scenic Spot on Baitarani river.
* **Bisoi** — Strategic Point.
* **Deokunda** — Scenic waterfall and religious site.
* **Haripur** — Historical Site.
* **Jamsola** — Welcome point on border.
* **Jashipur** — Entry Point to Similipal National Park.
* **Khiching** — Religious Centre, famous for Kichakeswari Temple.
* **Kuchai** — Historical Site.
* **Kuliana** — Historical Site.
* **Manatri** — Religious Centre.
* **Rairangpur** — Strategic Point.
* **Similipal** — Prominent National Park and Biosphere Reserve.
* **Samibrukhya** — Religious Centre and Scenic Spot.
* **Suleipat** — Dam Sight and scenic reservoir.
* **Meru Matha** — Scenic and Religious Centre.
* **Alkudar** — Scenic Centre and Waterfall.
* **Sitakunda** — Scenic Centre and Waterfall.

## Nuapara District Tourist Centres
* **Budhikomna** — Religious Centre, Pataleswar brick temple.
* **Nawapara** — District Headquarters.
* **Patalaganga** — Sacred religious site.
* **Patora** — Scenic Spot and Patora Dam.
* **Sindursil** — Scenic Spot.
* **Thipakhol** — Scenic Spot.
* **Yogimatha** — Ancient caves with historic Cave Paintings.

## Nawarangpur District Tourist Centres
* **Kelia** — Religious Centre.
* **Nawarangpur** — District Headquarters.
* **Papadahandi** — Religious Centre, Shiva temple.
* **Podagarh** — Historical Site and fort ruins.
* **Umarkote** — Religious Centre, Jagannath Temple and Podagarh fort nearby.

* **Baramul** — Scenic Spot, Satkosia Gorge.
* **Odagaon** — Religious Centre, Raghunath Temple.
* **Jamupatna** — Religious Centre.

## Puri District Tourist Centres
* **Astaranga** — Famous beach known for spectacular sunset views.
* **Balighai** — Sea Beach, Nuanai river mouth.
* **Baliharachandi** — Religious Centre and beach, temple of Goddess Harachandi.
* **Barala (Balunkeswar Pitha)** — Religious Centre, Balunkeswar Shiva temple.
* **Biswanath Hill** — Religious Centre, scenic hill.
* **Beleswar** — Scenic Sea Beach.
* **Brahmagiri** — Religious Centre, Alarnath Temple.
* **Chaurasi** — Religious Centre, Varahi Temple.
* **Chilika (Satapada)** — Lake resort famous for Irrawaddy Dolphin sightings.
* **Jahaniapira** — Religious Centre, shrine of Jahaniapira.
* **Kakatpur** — Religious Centre, Mangala Temple.
* **Konark** — UNESCO World Heritage Site Sun Temple.
* **Kuruma** — Ancient Buddhist Shrine.
* **Manikapatna** — Historical port site and religious center.
* **Pipili** — World-famous for Applique Handicrafts.
* **Puri** — Famous Char Dham site, Sri Jagannath Temple and Golden Beach.
* **Raghurajpur** — Heritage Crafts Village famous for Pattachitra paintings.
* **Ramachandi** — Scenic beach and temple on Puri-Konark Marine Drive.
* **Satyabadi (Sakhigopal)** — Religious Centre, Sakhigopal Temple.
* **Baligaon (Bhakta Dasia Smriti Pitha)** — Birth Place of Bhakta Dasia Bauri.
* **Maa Mangala Temple (Matrusakti Pitha)** — Religious Centre.

## Rayagada District Tourist Centres

* **Chatikona** — Scenic Spot and tribal market.

## Sambalpur District Tourist Centres
* **Chipilima** — Religious Centre and Ghanteswari Temple.
