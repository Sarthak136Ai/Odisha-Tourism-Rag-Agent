# Identified Tourist Centres in Odisha

This document lists the officially identified tourist centers in Odisha organized by district, place name, and its significance or importance.

## Angul District Tourist Centres
* **Angul** — District Headquarters.
* **Banarpal** — Strategic point.
* **Bhimkand** — Famous for the large reclining image of Sleeping Vishnu.
* **Binikei** — Religious Centre.
* **Bulajhara** — Scenic Spot.
* **Deulajhari** — Hot Spring and religious site.
* **Derjang** — Scenic view and Reservoir.
* **Handapapatrapada** — Scenic Spot.
* **Hingula Pitha** — Religious Centre / Shakti Pitha.
* **Khuludi** — Waterfall and scenic beauty.
* **Rengali** — Scenic Spot and site of Rengali Dam.
* **Talcher** — Industrial area and coal mines.
* **Tikarapara** — Scenic Spot, Gharial Crocodile Sanctuary, and wildlife in Satkosia Gorge.

## Balangir District Tourist Centres
* **Balangir** — District Headquarters.
* **Gaikhai M.I.P.** — Scenic Spot.
* **Harishankar** — Religious Centre on the slopes of Gandhamardan hills, famous for Harishankar Temple and waterfalls.
* **Jogisarada** — Religious Centre.
* **Patnagarh** — Religious Centre and ancient capital.
* **Ranipur Jharial** — Famous historical 64 Yogini Shrine (Chausathi Yogini Temple).
* **Saintala** — Religious Centre with historical relics.
* **Turekela** — Scenic Spot and wildlife.
* **Bhima Dunguri** — Religious and Historical Centre.
* **Kumuda Pahad** — Scenic and Religious Centre.

## Balasore District Tourist Centres
* **Ayodhya** — Religious Centre and major Archaeological Site.
* **Balaramgadi** — Sea Beach and active Fish Trading Centre.
* **Balasore** — District Headquarters and historical port town.
* **Bardhanpur** — Religious Centre.
* **Bhusandeswar** — Religious Centre, housing a massive Shiva Lingam.
* **Chandaneswar** — Famous Shiva temple and Religious Centre.
* **Chandipur** — Famous vanishing beach where the sea recedes up to 5 km during low tide.
* **Chasakhanda** — Baghajatin Memorial (historical site of freedom struggle).
* **Chowmukh Dagara** — Scenic Sea Beach.
* **Gud** — Religious Centre.
* **Inchudi** — Historical site famous for the Salt Satyagraha freedom movement.
* **Kasaphal** — Sea Beach.
* **Kupari** — Religious Centre.
* **Langaleswar** — Religious Centre.
* **Laxmananath** — Welcome point on the border.
* **Panchalingeswar** — Scenic Spot and hilltop shrine of Lord Shiva in the Nilgiri hills.
* **Raibania** — Historical group of medieval forts.
* **Remuna** — Famous religious center, home of Khirachora Gopinatha Temple.
* **Sajanagarh** — Religious Centre.
* **Talasari** — Scenic Sea Beach with casuarina trees.
* **Tripisagadia** — Religious Centre.
* **Tudigadia (Sahid Pitha)** — Historical Centre of freedom fighters.

* **Debrigarh** — Prominent Wildlife Sanctuary.
* **Devdarha** — Scenic Spot.

* **Barapali** — Famous Orissa Handloom center, birthplace of poet Gangadhar Meher.
* **Chikhidi** — Scenic Spot.
* **Bindhyabasini Hill (Pahad) Girigobardhanpitha-Bsaeikelagarh** — Religious, Historical and Scenic Centre.
